Appeal from the Center For Zakat Management (CZM)

Assalamu Alaikum.

CZM is a social institution established in 1993, aiming at promoting the obligation of Zakat and other donations as an Islamic economic tool for alleviating poverty and reduce socio economic disparity in our community. We mainly focus on sustainable uplift of the needy people in clusters in various districts of Bangladesh.

CZM has created a platform to promote Collective efforts for institutional management of Zakat facilitating private organizations and individuals to come forward so that the institution can mobilize and implement effectively the distribution of their Zakat, Sadaqa or any donation in a planned and effective manner as per the tenants of Islam.

We have a permanent manpower team of around 150 personnel deployed in many districts of Bangladesh. Our ongoing Flagship projects which Alhamdulillah are running with very good successes are shown on the left of this page.

During the times of national calamity and distress we strive to supplement in supporting the government efforts under our project “INSANIAT” which has brought us reputation and goodwill.

During Rohingya crises, our swift presence with medical clinics support and ambulance services were highly appreciated by not only our government and armed forces but from abroad as well.

Our projects are supervised and monitored by our Executive committee, Governing Board, Shariah Board and Advisory Council that comprised the highly reputed personalities of the society. They are listed in the attachment.
